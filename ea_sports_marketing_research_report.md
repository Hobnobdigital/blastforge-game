# EA Sports Marketing Careers: Comprehensive Research Report

## Executive Summary

EA Sports, a division of Electronic Arts, represents one of the most dynamic marketing environments in the gaming industry. With flagship franchises like EA SPORTS FC (formerly FIFA), Madden NFL, College Football, UFC, and F1, the marketing organization operates under the Experiences division led by Chief Experiences Officer David Tinson. This report provides actionable insights on open positions, leadership backgrounds, required qualifications, company culture, and strategic positioning for candidates seeking marketing roles at EA Sports.

## 2. Job Requirements, Skills & Qualifications

### Experience Requirements by Level

#### Entry-Level / Emerging Talent
- **EA SPORTS Academy**: For recent graduates and career changers
- **Marketing Internships**: Currently enrolled students or recent grads
- **Qualifications**: Bachelor's degree, passion for gaming/sports, basic marketing knowledge

#### Manager-Level (4-5 Years Experience)
- 4+ years strategic marketing at brand or agency
- Proven track record developing insight-driven campaigns
- Campaign management from concept through execution
- Stakeholder management across disciplines and time zones
- Experience with external agency management
- Budget management and performance tracking

#### Director-Level (7+ Years Experience)
- 7+ years in marketing, brand management, or agency leadership
- Team leadership and development experience
- Strategic planning and business planning expertise
- Multi-channel campaign orchestration
- Executive presentation and communication skills
- Deep understanding of gaming and/or sports culture

### Core Skills Required

**Technical Skills:**
- Data analysis and insight interpretation
- Campaign reporting and KPI measurement
- Creative brief development
- Deck-building and executive presentations
- Go-to-market strategy development
- Social media and content strategy
- Media planning and buying knowledge

**Industry Knowledge:**
- Understanding of North American sports culture
- Gaming industry trends (especially sports titles)
- Consumer behavior in digital entertainment
- Media landscape and engagement opportunities
- Esports ecosystem knowledge (for relevant roles)

**Soft Skills:**
- Collaborative mindset across cross-functional teams
- Curiosity and adaptability
- Strong written and verbal communication
- Cultural awareness and sensitivity
- Problem-solving under pressure
- Passion for sports and gaming

### Education
- Bachelor's degree required (Marketing, Communications, Business preferred)
- Advanced degrees (MBA) beneficial for senior roles
- Relevant certifications (digital marketing, analytics) a plus

### Additional Requirements
- Willingness to travel up to 25% for many roles
- Passion for EA Sports franchises strongly preferred
- Portfolio demonstrating campaign work

## 4. EA Sports Marketing Culture

### Core Values & Culture

**Storytelling & Brand Advocacy:**
- Focus on building immersive sports fan experiences
- Evergreen content strategies tied to major sports moments
- Partnership with athletes, leagues, and talent for authentic storytelling
- Cross-platform narrative development

**Cultural Connection:**
- "Highly culturalized" activations grounded in market research
- Deep understanding of consumer behavior and media trends
- Creating resonant, insight-led campaigns
- Balancing global brand consistency with local relevance

**Collaboration & Innovation:**
- Matrixed organization requiring cross-functional teamwork
- Working with agencies, partners, and internal teams across time zones
- Emphasis on curiosity and adaptability
- "Holistic approach" to wellness, creativity, and work-life balance

**Data-Driven Approach:**
- Strong focus on KPIs and impact measurement
- Campaign reporting and performance tracking
- Engagement and demand creation metrics
- Continuous optimization based on data insights

### What EA Looks For in Candidates

**Must-Haves:**
1. Demonstrated passion for sports and/or gaming
2. Track record of insight-driven campaign development
3. Ability to work in matrixed, cross-functional environments
4. Strong data literacy and analytical skills
5. Cultural awareness and adaptability

**Differentiators:**
- Direct experience with sports marketing or gaming
- Understanding of the EA Sports portfolio
- Innovative campaign examples in portfolio
- Community building and engagement experience
- Multi-market or international experience

**Red Flags to Avoid:**
- Generic marketing experience without specialized focus
- Lack of passion for the product/franchises
- Inability to demonstrate measurable campaign results
- Poor collaboration skills in complex environments

## 6. How to Position Yourself for EA Sports Marketing Roles

### Career Path Strategies

**For Recent Graduates:**
1. Apply to EA SPORTS Academy (cohort-based program)
2. Target Marketing Intern positions (Summer programs)
3. Build portfolio with gaming/sports marketing projects
4. Demonstrate deep knowledge of EA Sports franchises

**For Mid-Career Professionals (3-7 years):**
1. Gain sports or gaming marketing experience at agencies or brands
2. Build measurable campaign results portfolio
3. Develop data analytics and insights capabilities
4. Network through industry events and LinkedIn
5. Target Manager-level roles first

**For Senior Professionals (7+ years):**
1. Demonstrate team leadership and strategic planning
2. Show cross-functional collaboration experience
3. Build executive presence and communication skills
4. Consider MBA or advanced credentials
5. Target Senior Manager or Director roles

### Application Best Practices

**Resume Tips:**
- Lead with measurable results ("Increased engagement by X%", "Managed $X budget")
- Highlight gaming/sports experience prominently
- Include specific EA Sports franchise knowledge
- Demonstrate cross-functional collaboration
- Show international or multi-market experience

**Portfolio Development:**
- Include campaign work from insight through execution
- Show creative briefs and strategic frameworks
- Demonstrate data analysis and reporting
- Highlight community building and engagement
- Include presentations or decks for executive audiences

**Networking Strategy:**
- Follow EA marketing leaders on LinkedIn
- Engage with EA Sports content authentically
- Attend gaming industry events (E3, Gamescom, GDC)
- Join gaming marketing communities
- Consider informational interviews

**Interview Preparation:**
- Deeply research specific franchises relevant to the role
- Understand current campaigns and industry trends
- Prepare campaign critiques and improvement suggestions
- Demonstrate passion for the product
- Show cultural awareness and adaptability

### Key Differentiators

**What Makes Candidates Stand Out:**
1. **Passion + Knowledge**: Deep understanding of EA Sports games
2. **Results**: Quantifiable campaign impact
3. **Cultural Fit**: Alignment with collaborative, innovative culture
4. **Growth Mindset**: Willingness to learn and adapt
5. **Sports/Gaming Credibility**: Authentic connection to the industry

**Skills to Develop:**
- Gaming industry analytics and metrics
- Sports marketing campaign development
- Cross-cultural marketing experience
- Influencer and creator economy knowledge
- Emerging platform expertise (TikTok, streaming)
