# Lululemon 2023 Global Wellbeing Report - Design Analysis

## Format
- **Dimensions:** 960 x 540 pts (16:9 landscape)
- **Pages:** 58 total (we're creating 10)
- **Created in:** PowerPoint

## Color Palette
- **Primary Accent:** Red (#E31837 or similar)
- **Black:** Backgrounds, header boxes
- **White:** Text overlays, backgrounds
- **Light Gray:** #F5F5F5 for section backgrounds

## Typography
- **Font:** Sans-serif (Calibri or similar)
- **Styles:**
  - Large headings: Bold, white on images
  - Statistics: Bold, red, 60-80pt
  - Body text: Regular, 12-14pt
  - Vertical sidebar text: Rotated 90Â°

## Page Templates

### 1. Cover Page (Page 1)
- Full-bleed lifestyle image
- Large white text: "Global" (top left), main word (center), "Report" (below)
- Year in bottom right
- Logo vertically on left side with brand name

### 2. Table of Contents (Page 2)
- Left 60%: Image collage on black background
- Right 40%: White background, "Table of Contents" vertical text
- List items with page numbers

### 3. Section Divider (Pages 3, 6)
- Full-bleed lifestyle image
- Large white heading text (lower left)
- Black sidebar on right with vertical section name
- Logo top left

### 4. Content with Images (Page 4)
- White background right side for text
- Large lifestyle image center/left
- Small images on left side
- Red vertical accent lines for callout boxes
- Black sidebar right

### 5. Data Visualization (Page 5)
- White background
- Infographic (map, chart)
- Red accent boxes for key stats
- Vertical section title left side

### 6. Statistics Grid (Pages 7-10)
- Header: Image strip with text overlay OR clean white with heading
- 2-3 column layout
- Black header boxes for sub-sections
- Red statistics (large numbers)
- Supporting body text
- Light gray background

## Consistent Elements
- **Logo:** Top left on all content pages
- **Page numbers:** Bottom left
- **Right sidebar:** Black background, vertical text (section name)
- **Red accents:** Statistics, callout text, accent lines

## Image Style
- Lifestyle photography
- Diverse people
- Natural settings
- Professional quality
- Authentic, not overly posed
