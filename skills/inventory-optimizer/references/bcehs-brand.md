# BCEHS Brand Guidelines

## Colors

**Primary Colors:**
- **BCEHS Blue:** #003366 (RGB: 0, 51, 102)
- **BCEHS Navy:** #002855 (RGB: 0, 40, 85)
- **White:** #FFFFFF

**Accent Colors:**
- **Light Blue:** #5B9BD5 (RGB: 91, 155, 213)
- **Gray:** #7F7F7F (RGB: 127, 127, 127)
- **Light Gray:** #D9D9D9 (RGB: 217, 217, 217)

## Typography

**Headers:** Arial Bold or Calibri Bold  
**Body Text:** Arial or Calibri Regular  
**Size Guide:**
- Title: 16pt Bold
- Section Headers: 14pt Bold
- Body: 11pt Regular
- Table Text: 10pt Regular

## Logo Usage

- Logo must be included on all official documents
- Clear space around logo equal to height of "E" in BCEHS
- Never distort or recolor logo
- Use on white background or BCEHS Blue background (white version)

## Document Formatting

**Headers:**
- Include "BCEHS Talent Acquisition" in header
- Date and document title

**Tables:**
- Header row: BCEHS Blue background with white text
- Alternating row colors: White and Light Gray
- Border color: Gray
- Bold text for headers

**Footers:**
- Page numbers
- "Confidential - Internal Use Only" if applicable
