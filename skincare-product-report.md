## Ingredient Analysis

### Tier 1: Brightening Agents

**Alpha Arbutin** (YouthHero AM Serum)
- Mechanism: Tyrosinase inhibitor
- Efficacy: Reduces melanin production by 50-70% in clinical studies
- Safety Profile: Well-tolerated; suitable for all skin types
- Optimal Concentration: 1-2%

**Azelaic Acid** (The Ordinary 10% + Cleansing Bar)
- Mechanism: Inhibits tyrosinase; anti-inflammatory
- Efficacy: Clinically proven for melasma and PIH
- Additional Benefits: Antimicrobial (acne), rosacea treatment
- Optimal Concentration: 10-20%

**Kojic Acid** (Cleansing Bar)
- Mechanism: Chelates copper in tyrosinase
- Efficacy: Comparable to hydroquinone without sensitization
- Synergy: Enhanced when paired with Vitamin C

### Tier 2: Renewal Agents

**Retinoid 1%** (YouthHero PM Serum)
- Mechanism: Accelerates cell turnover; collagen stimulation
- Efficacy: Gold standard for anti-aging and PIH
- Considerations: Gradual introduction recommended; SPF mandatory

## Routine Protocol

### Morning Routine
1. **Cleanse** — Even Tone Cleansing Bar (60 seconds, lukewarm water)
2. **Treat** — YouthHero AM Serum (3-4 drops, pat into skin)
3. **Optional** — Azelaic Acid 10% (thin layer on problem areas)
4. **Protect** — Complexion Moisturizer SPF 30 (generous application)

### Evening Routine
1. **Cleanse** — Even Tone Cleansing Bar
2. **Treat** — YouthHero PM Serum (1% Retinoid)
3. **Massage** — Gua Sha Tool (5-10 minutes, upward strokes)
4. **Moisturize** — As needed
